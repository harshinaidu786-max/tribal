Tribal Handicrafts Hub

Connecting tribal artisans with customers — sell authentic, value‑added handicrafts and share the story behind every item.

How it works

  1. Local tribal artisans submit handcrafted items through our intake form.
  2. We curate, photograph, and list the items on this marketplace.
  3. Customers buy directly from the site; profits are shared transparently with artisans.

This demo page includes a client-side submission form so you can preview intake and listing without a backend. When ready we can integrate a server, storage and payment gateway (Razorpay/Stripe).
